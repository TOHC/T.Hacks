What is THACKS?

The best October weekend you'll ever have. A festival of three hundred hackers, designers and entrepreneurs.

THacks is a 24 hour Hackathon located in the heart of Toronto. There, you'll spent the weekend designing, building and pitching an amazing hack - whether it be a Uber for something else, a neural network or a hardware project.

TL;DR it's gonna be a lot of fun, free food, learning and friends in one weekend. It's not just for nerds - it's for you. Reach.
