What is T.Hacks?

T.Hacks is a free 12 hour hackathon for students of all ages, proficiencies, and interests. The event offers an opportunity to tailor your own personalized hackathon experience, with access to a variety of workshops and networking opportunities. Meals will be provided for all participants, as well as swag and prizes from leading companies in the industry.

Why Attend?

Attending T.Hacks grants you the opportunity to meet and work with like-minded students and professionals. Peers and experienced mentors attending the event will be available to answer questions or provide advice on any personal endeavour, while free food and tons of prizes will be up for grabs throughout the day. Whether you are already an experienced hacker, or have never written a line of code, there is something for everyone at T.Hacks!
