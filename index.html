T.Hacks

October 22, 2016

What is T.Hacks?

Hosted on October 22nd, 2016, in Toronto, Canada, T.Hacks is a 12-hour hackathon run by the Toronto Hacker Club for programmers in high school and university. The event offers an extensive selection of workshops, appealing to participants of all levels, including introductions to Python and Java for beginners, as well as Android and Node.js for attendees seeking more advanced programming skills.

Why Attend?

Attending T.Hacks not only grants you the opportunity to meet and work with like-minded students, professionals and mentors, it invites you to tailor your own 12-hour, personalized hackathon experience. Peers and experienced mentors attending the event will be available for questions, networking and advice on programming endeavours, while free food and tons of prizes will be up for grabs for the duration of the day.
